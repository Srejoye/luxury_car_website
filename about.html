About Us

Ride Techs

For over two decades, Ride Techs has set the bar high in luxury automotive excellence. Established 22 years ago, we are dedicated to blending the finest craftsmanship with state-of-the-art technology, bringing you vehicles that are more than just cars—they're statements of prestige and innovation. Our journey is driven by a passion for pushing boundaries and redefining what luxury means in the world of mobility. Each Ride Techs vehicle is crafted to provide an unmatched driving experience, embodying our commitment to quality, performance, and timeless elegance.

Our Mission

To deliver an unparalleled luxury automotive experience by blending cutting-edge technology, refined craftsmanship, and exceptional customer service, ensuring every ride redefines excellence. Our Journey Through Time

2003

A Dream Takes Flight

In a small showroom in Noida, with just three gleaming luxury vehicles and an abundance of passion, Ride Techs was born. Our founders dared to dream of revolutionizing India's luxury car market, armed with nothing but determination and a vision of excellence.

2008

Rising to New Heights

As our reputation for excellence grew, so did our ambitions. We unveiled our stunning flagship showroom, becoming the talk of the town. This year also marked our ascension as the exclusive dealer for several coveted premium brands. But our crowning achievement? The launch of our signature personalization service, allowing clients to turn their automotive dreams into reality.

2015

Pioneering the Future

In a bold move that left competitors in awe, we unveiled our state-of-the-art service center - a temple of automotive care and precision. But we didn't stop there. Ride Techs made history by introducing India's first virtual reality car customization experience, blending technology and luxury in ways never seen before.

2024

A Legacy of Luxury

Today, Ride Techs stands as a colossus in the luxury automotive world. With multiple locations across India, a breathtaking collection of over 200 premium vehicles, and a reputation that precedes us, we've become synonymous with automotive excellence. But this isn't the end of our story - it's merely the latest chapter in our ongoing saga of redefining luxury.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for luxury and innovation in the automotive industry, transforming how people perceive and experience luxury mobility. We envision a future where Ride Techs leads the way in sustainable luxury, utilizing advanced technologies to enhance both environmental responsibility and driving pleasure. By consistently embracing progress and pushing creative boundaries, we aspire to shape the next era of luxury vehicles, where elegance, power, and sustainability coexist in perfect harmony.
